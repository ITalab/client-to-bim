üìã Abstract

Translating heterogeneous, client-authored textual requirements into constructible, information-rich models constitutes a primary impediment to digital transformation in early design phases. This paper introduces an end-to-end automation pipeline that couples advanced Natural Language Processing with Building Information Modeling to dynamically interpret design intent from user inputs and instantiate corresponding BIM assemblies.
